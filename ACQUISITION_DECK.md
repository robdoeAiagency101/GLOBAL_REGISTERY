**18 million** children globally are blind or severely visually impaired.

Current assistive tech:
- ❌ Centralized data (single point of failure)
- ❌ No cryptographic verification

- ❌ No real-time environmental truth
- ❌ Limited to pre-programmed routes

These kids can't navigate independently. They can't verify the information they're given.

## The Solution

**E14 Oracle** creates a **decentralized, cryptographically verified environmental truth layer** that:

✅ Combines multiple satellite sources (BOM, Himawari, GOES, Meteosat)
✅ Uses XYO witness mesh for cryptographic verification
✅ Requires Byzantine consensus (K≥0.99) before execution
✅ Provides tamper-proof, auditable data
✅ Enables independent navigation for sensory-impaired users

**No single entity controls the truth. Math does.**
